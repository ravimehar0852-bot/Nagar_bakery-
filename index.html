Our Story

More Than a Bakery

Nestled near the Shri Kamkheda Balaji Mandir in Kamkheda, Rajasthan, Nagar Dudh Deyari & Cake Bakery has been a cornerstone of the community — bringing together the finest fresh dairy and the most delicious baked treats.

We believe in the old ways: pure milk, handcrafted dough, no shortcuts. Every loaf, every cake, every spoonful of paneer carries the warmth of a family that loves what it does.
